Hardware & Spatial Data

The next frontier in data collection. Continuous, consented capture of real-world audio, video, and spatial information.

The Vision

Smartphones capture moments. Wearables capture life.

AR glasses and spatial computing devices will generate categories of data that don't exist today. First-person video, spatial audio, eye tracking, environmental mapping.

Harbor is building the infrastructure to ingest, process, and license this data — before the hardware becomes mainstream.
