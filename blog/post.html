Welcome to HARBOR: The Future of Media-Native AI Data

Introducing a new platform for sourcing, annotating, and licensing premium media datasets for AI training.

AO
HARBOR Team Engineering

The AI industry is facing a data crisis. As models grow more powerful, the need for high-quality, ethically-sourced training data has never been greater. HARBOR is our answer to this challenge.

The Problem

Today's AI companies face a difficult choice: scrape data without permission and risk legal action, or spend months negotiating individual licensing deals. Neither approach scales.

Meanwhile, content creators—photographers, videographers, musicians—see their work used to train AI models without compensation or consent.

Our Solution

HARBOR is a media-native data infrastructure platform that connects:

  • Contributors who earn money by licensing their content
  • AI companies who access premium, licensed datasets via API
  • Annotators who add value through expert labeling
"We believe the future of AI training data is permissioned, tracked, and compensated."

Key Features

Provenance from Day Zero

Every piece of content in HARBOR is tracked from the moment of capture. We know who created it, when, and under what terms it can be used.

Frame-Addressable Storage

Our Layer-1 video network allows you to access specific frames, time ranges, or scenes—not just entire videos.

Graph RAG for Media

Using Cognee, we've built a knowledge graph that understands relationships between scenes, objects, and actions across millions of hours of video.

Rights-Respecting Licensing

Enterprises can license datasets with confidence, knowing exactly what they can use the data for, in which territories, and for how long.

What's Next

We're launching with video and audio datasets, with image and text support coming soon. Book a demo to see HARBOR in action.
