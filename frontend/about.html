About

Building the Data Layer for Media AI

Harbor is infrastructure for audio and video AI — from collection to annotation to deployment.

Mission

AI systems need high-quality audio and video data at unprecedented scale. The infrastructure to collect, annotate, and license it responsibly doesn't exist.

Harbor is building that infrastructure. Not as a feature layer on top of cloud storage, but as a vertically integrated system designed from the ground up for media-native AI.

We believe the companies that own data infrastructure will shape how AI develops.

Philosophy

Infrastructure First

We own the stack from media ingest to frame-level addressing. Controlling the primitives makes everything else possible.

Media Native

Audio and video are not text with extra steps. They require different infrastructure, annotation, and delivery.

Rights at the Core

Licensing and consent are infrastructure requirements. Every piece of data carries its provenance.

APIs Over UI

Our primary customers are machines and developers. UI exists for visibility; APIs are the product.
