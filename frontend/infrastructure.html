Why Infrastructure Matters

Most AI data platforms rent infrastructure from cloud providers and add a labeling layer on top. Harbor is different.

We own the complete stack from media ingest to frame-level addressing. This isn't about being full-stack for its own sake — it's the foundation of defensibility.

When you control the infrastructure, you control latency, cost structure, and the ability to build features impossible on rented platforms.
